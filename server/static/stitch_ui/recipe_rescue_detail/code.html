THE PREPARATION

1

Prepare the Glaze

Whisk together white miso paste, mirin, sake, and a touch of brown sugar until smooth. The consistency should be thick but pourable.

2

Marinate Salmon

Coat the salmon fillets generously with the glaze. Let them sit for at least 15 minutes at room temperature to absorb the deep umami notes.

3

High-Heat Broil

Place salmon under a preheated broiler. Cook for 6-8 minutes until the glaze bubbles and turns a deep, dark caramel color with slight charring on the edges.
